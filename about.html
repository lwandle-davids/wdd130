Experience the thrill of white water rafting on the mighty Zambezi River! Our team is passionate about adventure and safety, offering unforgettable rafting trips for all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned rafter, we have something for everyone.

History

Founded in 2010, Zimbabwe Rafting Adventures has been providing thrilling and safe rafting experiences for over a decade, introducing thousands to the excitement of white water rafting in Bulawayo.

About Us

Our mission is to provide safe, exciting, and memorable rafting experiences while showcasing the natural beauty of Zimbabwe. We are committed to environmental sustainability and supporting our local community.

Why Choose Us?

  • Certified and experienced guides
  • Top-quality safety equipment
  • Customizable trips for families, groups, and solo adventurers
  • Stunning views of the Zambezi River and surrounding wildlife
